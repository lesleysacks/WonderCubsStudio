It was a bright, sunny morning.

Leo the Lion smiled.

"Let's learn our ABCs!" said Leo.

Ruby the Rabbit held a shiny red apple.

"A is for Apple!"

Everyone smiled.

Milo the Monkey bounced a big blue ball.

"B is for Ball!"

Everyone cheered.

Ellie the Elephant hugged a cute little cat.

"C is for Cat!"

Everyone clapped.

Leo found a happy brown dog.

"D is for Dog!"

Everyone barked, "Woof! Woof!"

Ruby pointed to a big elephant.

"E is for Elephant!"

Everyone laughed.

Leo said,

"A, B, C, D, E!"

"Can you say them with us?"

Everyone said together,

"A, B, C, D, E!"

"Great job!"

The friends danced happily through the sunny forest.

Learning is fun with friends!
